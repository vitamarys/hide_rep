about the Palm Jumeirah

About the area
Featured units

Man-made island located off the coast of Dubai in the Arabian Gulf and is one of three islands in the Palm Islands project, along with Palm Jebel Ali and Palm Deira

This remarkable and unique destination was created in the early 2000s as part of a massive development project to increase Dubai's tourism and real estate industries and has become a symbol of the city’s ambition and innovation.

Palm Jumeirah is often referred to as the “eighth wonder of the world” due to its impressive engineering and design, built using reclaimed sand and rock that was dredged from the Gulf and shaped like a palm tree with a trunk, fronds, and a crescent-shaped island that acts as a breakwater. The island is connected to the mainland by a 17,000-foot-long bridge and is visible from space.

Stunning views

Residents and visitors can enjoy stunning views of the Arabian Gulf while relaxing in a luxurious setting or engaging in water sports activities.

5-Star Dining

Palm Jumeirah is a food lover’s paradise where guests can indulge in an unforgettable culinary experience, with a range of remarkable restaurants.

One&Only

Residents can enjoy a private beach, a infinity swimming pool, and a state-of-the-art fitness centre, and dine at STAY by Yannick Alléno with two Michelin stars.
